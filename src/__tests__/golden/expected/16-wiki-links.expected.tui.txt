 Plain wiki link: Other Page (#/page/other_page) points to a related note.      
                                                                                
 Aliased wiki link: the roadmap (#/page/project_roadmap) uses a custom label.   
                                                                                
 Inline mix with regular links: see Index (#/page/index) or visit the docs (    
 https://example.com).                                                          
                                                                                
 Multiple in a paragraph: refer to Concept A (#/page/concept_a), Concept B (#/  
 page/concept_b), or Concept C (#/page/concept_c) for details.
